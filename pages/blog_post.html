VIETNAM PLACE TO LIVE.

HO CHI MINH CITY, Biggest city in Vietnam and it's the Economy capital of the country. If you want to enjoy hour holiday with a morden lifstyle, mix-cultures and International food but a cheap living-cost so Ho Chi Minh city will be the best idea that you can think about.

Hanoi, Vietnam Capital with 36 antique towns will help you imagine about an Old Vietnam.

Hue, DaNang, NhaTrang, HoiAn there are some more city that you MUST visit when you arrive to Vietnam for a long and unforgettable trip.

THINGS TO DO

Traditional Food around the country!

  • Pho(Beef Noddle)Vietnam Best Food. You can find anywhere in Vietnam that selling this kind of dish and you will find out that the tastes are not same in most of the place you visit.
  • Bun Cha Hanoi, The other food MUST try.
  • And more and more traditional food along the country that you need to spend months to try.

Landscapes around Vietnam that you will love!

  • Quang Binh province. With more than Hundreds caves and the most popular caves are Phong Nha-Ke Bang and Son Doong(The Biggest cave in the world)
  • Dalat city, The highlander with thousands kinds for flowers and the city is very quiet and peacefull
  • Nha Trang city, the Beach city with many beautiful beaches and warm sea water.
  • HA GIANG, SAPA, MU CANG CHAY and more and more beautiful places are waiting for your explorer.

Suggested Tourist Company: Saigon Tourist

Saigontourist was set up just after the end of the Vietnam War to take charge of many of Saigon's hotels and restaurants. It diversified somewhat in the 1990s, but mainly remained focused on its core businesses in the hotel, tourism, and entertainment sectors, which were growing rapidly. It also developed interests in banking and distribution, becoming the leading shareholder in the joint-stock commercial bank Oricom Bank and the distributor of Ford in Vietnam. In 1995, Saigontourist was the seventeenth largest company by turnover in Ho Chi Minh City.
